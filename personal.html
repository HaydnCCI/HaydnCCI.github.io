Innovative Ideas

With open source, development platform and online courses. The abundance of information incubate ideas that can potentially impact lives of millions!

Ubiquitous of Electronics

The distribution of electronic devices has infused into our everyday life, rescue us from drudgery and redefined what can be done during our lifetime!

Moore's Law 2.0

Explosive growth of data and halt of Moore's Law bring forth the Moore's Law 2.0! If Moore's Law has enable faster computation, Moore's Law 2.0 yields smarter devices!

Personalization of Products

AI can characterise user behavior, preference and needs at a level beyond most people can imagine. This yields Personalization user experience and products. With this dynamic setting, 1-to-1 touchpoints, there's no need for "One size fits all"!

Commercialization of AI

After the A.I. winter in the 80s' and 90s', now it's time to shine with: (1)Moore's Law (2)Moore's Law 2.0 (3)The Cloud. Focusing on R&D in narrow-AI has proven success in enhancing value to user experience, saving cost and lowering risks. Resulted in A.I. companies domination.

Digitalization of Daily Life

With the support of Fiber-optic communication and the imminent 5G technology, IoT and others will further digitalize our daily life so that we can have more time, health and energy to focus on what matters for us!
